Marge Bouvier Simpson

Age : 34

Occupation : Mother and houswife

Voice : Julie Kavner

Favorite Magasine : Sponge & Vaccum


The everloving mother of the family. She is the typical mother, she allways has to look after everyone, including Homer.

Although she almost allway make good judgement, makes the right discisions and teaches the children the right things, sometimes she is proved wrong by Lisa.

Marge and Maggie are almost allways together. Since Maggie is so young, wherever Marge goes, so does Maggie.
